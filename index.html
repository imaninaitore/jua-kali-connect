A website description, or meta description, is a short HTML snippet (typically 120–160 characters) summarizing a page's content, displayed under the title in search engine results (SERPs). It acts as a marketing tool to encourage clicks, improves user experience, and helps search engines understand the page topic, though it is not a direct ranking factor.
